Stray Cats & Dogs

A parent was bitten by a stray cat on campus and needed rabies shots — this is a safety plan, not just a discussion.

Home Social Finance Section 3 Section 4 Stray Cats & Dogs

What's agreed so far

No culling or poisoning — ruled out. The approach is prevention: remove what attracts the animals and seal up how they get in.

  • Secure the campus perimeter — fill gaps in the rear fence
  • No dogs on campus, brought by parents or teachers
  • Reduce food scraps — clean-up drive + educate kids and teachers
  • Screen classroom windows so cats (and insects) can't get in
  • Monitor for a few months, then reassess
